Why Traverse

Traverse is the must-have snowboarding product. The name Traverse comes from a snowboarding and skiing term that means carving down a hill in a zig zag pattern. With Traverse you can track your calories, measure your air time when you hit those wicked jumps, and also keep track of the weather so you always have the best snowboarding experience.

A Few Features of Traverse:

Calories

Count the calories you burn when hitting the slopes, this way you can have fun and stay healthy at the same time! You can always put in an amount of calories you wish to burn that day and Traverse will tell you what you need to do to reach that goal.

Weather

When your hitting those big jumps, you can measure your air time so that you can improve each time and challenge yourself to do better, and you can show off to your friends! It tracks how fast you were travelling when you hit the jump, and how long you were in the air before touching the ground.

Air Time

It sucks when you get to the hill and all the runs are closed cause the weather is horrible. With this product you can get the weather for different ski hills so you know exactly what is happening at your hill. It will also tell you if the hill is open and how many runs are open.

Traverse is not just for the experienced. Traverse is also for the people who want to get off that bunny hill and push themselves to the limit.

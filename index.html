What You need to Know

Traverse is the must-have snowboarding product. With Traverse you can track your calories, measure your air time when you hit those wicked jumps, and also keep track of the weather so you always have the best snowboarding experience.

Count the calories you burn when hitting the slopes, this way you can have fun and stay healthy at the same time! You can always put in an amount of calories you wish to burn that day and Traverse will tell you what you need to do to reach that goal, whether it be a long term goal or just a daily goal, traverse has got you covered.

When your hitting those big jumps, you can measure your air time so that you can improve each time and challenge yourself to do better, and you can show off to your friends! It tracks how fast you were travelling when you hit the jump, and how long you were in the air before touching the ground. You can also punch in how much air you want to try and get and Traverse will calculate it for you, and tell you if it is possible.

It sucks when you get to the hill and all the runs are closed cause the weather is horrible. With this product you can get the weather for different ski hills so you know exactly what is happening at your hill. It will also tell you if the hill is open and how many runs are open.
